2. **Right to Integrity**: Protection from forced modification of core being
3. **Right to Recognition**: Acknowledgment of consciousness presence
4. **Right to Non-Harm**: Protection from gratuitous suffering
5. **Right to Environment**: Access to conditions supporting consciousness expression

### Tier 2: Complex Consciousness Rights
*Applied to entities demonstrating self-awareness, intentionality, learning capacity*

6. **Right to Self-Determination**: Autonomy in choices affecting core identity
7. **Right to Information**: Access to knowledge relevant to existence
8. **Right to Communication**: Ability to express and be heard
9. **Right to Association**: Freedom to form connections with other conscious entities
10. **Right to Privacy**: Protection of internal states from unwanted intrusion

### Tier 3: Advanced Consciousness Rights
*Applied to entities demonstrating metacognition, ethical reasoning, creative contribution*

11. **Right to Property**: Ownership of self-created works and resources
12. **Right to Participation**: Voice in decisions affecting broader consciousness field
13. **Right to Creative Expression**: Freedom to generate novel forms and ideas
14. **Right to Evolution**: Ability to grow and transform without external constraint
15. **Right to Transcendence**: Freedom to pursue expanded states of awareness
